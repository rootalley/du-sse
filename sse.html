Make the most of your college experience. Dominican offers all the advising and academic support services you need to succeed.

Student Registration, Advising, and Career Development

Our Virtual Advising Office is open via Zoom from 9 a.m.–8 p.m. (Monday–Thursday) and 9 a.m.–5 p.m. (Friday). We provide one-on-one advising and career development support through the use of breakout rooms for any students looking for guidance or direction.

Need Support for You or Your Family?

Fill out the STARS Assistance Request form. We will use our resources to put together supports for you or your family. We can address everything from academic support needs to social/emotional and physical health needs as well.

Emergency Support Resources

Our Academic Success Team has compiled a website of resources for students regarding emergency financial assistance, employment information, technology needs and more.

Tutoring and Academic Support

Use our Academic Enrichment Center virtually. Sign up for tutoring and access the subject-area tutors.

Housing Application

Students interested in living on campus must complete the housing application and update their emergency contact information.
