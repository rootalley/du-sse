Make the most of your college experience. Dominican offers all the advising and academic support services you need to succeed.

Financial Assistance Application

Students who have experienced an unexpected personal circumstance during the Spring 2020 semester that prevents them from paying tuition may apply for financial assistance.

Student Registration, Advising, and Career Development

Our Virtual Advising Office (Links to an external site.) is open via Zoom from 9 a.m.–8 p.m. (Monday–Thursday) and 9 a.m.–5 p.m. (Friday). We provide one-on-one advising and career development support through the use of breakout rooms for any students looking for guidance or direction.

Our Career Development staff has compiled a flyer of support resources to assist you through the COVID-19 outbreak.

Need Support for You or Your Family?

Fill out the STARS Assistance Request (Links to an external site.) form. We will use our resources to put together supports for you or your family. We can address everything from academic support needs to social/emotional and physical health needs as well.

Emergency Support Resources

Our Academic Success Team has compiled a website of resources (Links to an external site.) for students regarding emergency financial assistance, employment information, technology needs and more.

Wellness Center Resources

The Wellness Center is providing telehealth sessions for students in need of counseling or other support. To make appointments, students should call 708-524-6229 or visit the telehealth website (Links to an external site.).
